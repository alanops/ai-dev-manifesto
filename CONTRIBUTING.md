# 🙌 Contributing to the AI-Dev Manifesto

Thanks for wanting to contribute! This manifesto is a living document — feel free to open issues or PRs if you:

## 💡 Ways to Contribute

- **Improve clarity** - Suggest better language or structure
- **Add real-world examples** - Share stories of these principles in action
- **Adapt for different contexts** - Help make it work for different team types or industries
- **Fix issues** - Spot something that could be clearer or more inclusive
- **Translate** - Help make this available in other languages

## 🚀 How to Contribute

1. **Open an Issue** for discussion before major changes
2. **Fork the repo** and create a branch for your changes
3. **Keep changes focused** - one principle or improvement per PR
4. **Test your changes** - read them out loud, share with a colleague
5. **Be respectful** - this manifesto serves diverse teams and working styles

## 🎯 What We're Looking For

- **Practical wisdom** over theoretical perfection
- **Inclusive language** that works for developers at all levels
- **Real-world tested** principles, not just nice-sounding ideas
- **Concise, actionable** guidance

## ❌ What We're Not Looking For

- Technology-specific advice (this should work for any stack)
- Rigid rules or processes
- Lengthy philosophical discussions
- Company-specific policies

## 🤝 Community Guidelines

- Be kind and constructive in discussions
- Assume good intent from contributors
- Focus on making the manifesto more helpful, not just different
- Remember that teams have diverse contexts and constraints

Let's build better developer culture — together! 🚀
